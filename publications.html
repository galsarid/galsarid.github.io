    PhD thesis:

    In my thesis work I followed the thermo-chemical evolution of modeled nuclei, for comets, centaurs and Kuiper belt objects.
    This was done through the development and application of a 1D and quasi-3D numerical code, which solves the heat transfer and gas flow equations in a porous multi-component grid, with self-consistent treatment of interior and exteior boundary conditions.
